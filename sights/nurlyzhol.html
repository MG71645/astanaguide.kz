Nurlyzhol Boulevard

Water-Green Boulevard (Boulevard Nurzhol) is located in the new administrative and business centre of Astana, on the left bank of the River Ishim. This construction has a pedestrian zone, extending from the president's residence Ak-Orda to the shopping centre Khan Shatyr. There are several architectural sites along the boulevard, which were built by famous Russian and foreign architects. Parking facility is on the lower level, the average level is occupied by commercial pavilions, business offices. The pedestrian area, decorated with flower beds, fountains, beautiful floral walkways with benches occupies the upper zone
