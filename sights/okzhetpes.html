Ush-Kyz, Ok-Zhetpes and Zhumbaktas Rocks

"Ush-kyz" (In translation from the Kazakh – “three women”), "Ok-Zhetpes" (In translation from the Kazakh - "The arrow will not reach". On the top of this rock you will see a stone that looks like an elephant) and "Zhumbaktas" (In translation from the Kazakh - “mountain riddle ") rocks, each having the surprising legends reflecting history and identity of the Kazakh people
